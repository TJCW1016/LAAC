Medical Applications of Computer Science

Introduction to medical computer applications of computer science

Out of all the ways technology has improved in the last few centries, none are as important as the technological advances in medicine improving the lives of humans across the globe. From the early invention of the X-ray machines to advances in surgical practices, technology has made us healthier and increased the quality of life of people whom are made avalaible to the healthcare. As the technology improves as we move further into the 21st century, we continue to develop technologies that cure illnesses and improve our quality of life.

In the last few decades the rate of expansion in technology has increased exponentially, with new inventions & techniques constantly being improved & made better. Technology is only going to improve in the upcoming years & the medical practices will become more efficent and more efficent.

The benefits of computer science in the medical sector

  • Improves health care quality and effectiveness.
  • Increases health care productivity and efficiency.
  • Prevents medical errors and increases health care accuracy and procedural correctness.
  • Reduces health care costs;
  • Increases administrative efficiencies and healthcare work processes.
  • Decreases paperwork and unproductive or idle work time.
  • Extends real-time communications of health informatics among health care professionals.
  • Expands access to affordable care.
